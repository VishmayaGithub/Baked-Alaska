Tools you need to make this desert :
 > Oven mitts
 > Plastic wrap
 > A spoon
 > Glass mixing bowls
 > Electric mixer
 > Palette knives or a spatula

What ingredients you need :
 > Two cups of sugar
 > Ay flavour icecream
 > Chocolate cake (can use any other flavour)
 > Four small eggs
 > Cream of tartar

 *You will also need a oven
